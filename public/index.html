Our Mission

When your candle is burning, you need to know the amount that is left. How Much Of Candle uses your webcam to monitor burning candles, and automatically calculates how much of the candle remains.

Helpful notifications over your social media feeds announce the percentage of candle left, ensuring that you and your friends are the first to know how far you’ve traveled on your candle journey.

  • Innovative machine vision algorithms teach your computer how to see fire.
  • Tracks percentage of candle burnt and estimates remaining candle duration.
  • Automatically shares your candle’s story with followers on Twitter and Facebook. (Pro Users Only)
  • Provides peace of mind while leaving candles unattended.

What is a Candle?

A candle is a convenient cylinder optimized for sustaining a small open flame. Simply ignite the rope of the candle, then sit back and enjoy the resulting light and heat. The candle will gradually shrink as its flesh melts away, and eventually the flame will turn off on its own. To extinguish the flame sooner, spit air or water from your mouth onto the fire.

How Much Of Candle is compatible with a wide variety of common household candles. Candles can be purchased.
